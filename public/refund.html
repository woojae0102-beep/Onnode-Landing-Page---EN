Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2025

Important: This refund policy has been prepared in accordance with the Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, considering the characteristics of digital services.

Article 1 (Overview of Refund Policy)

OnNode prioritizes customer satisfaction and operates a fair and transparent refund policy. While there may be some limitations due to the nature of digital content and services, we actively process refunds when there are legitimate reasons.

All payments, billing, subscription management, and refunds are processed and managed by Paddle.com Market Limited (“Paddle”), who acts as the Merchant of Record for all transactions. Refund eligibility, processing procedures, and timelines are determined in accordance with Paddle’s policies and applicable consumer protection laws. The Company does not directly process payments or store full payment details.

Article 2 (Refundable Conditions)

2.1 Full Refund Available (Within 7 days from purchase date)

Full refunds are available in the following cases:

  • When the service has not been used at all
  • When service use was impossible due to system errors
  • When the actual service significantly differs from advertised content
  • When service provision is impossible due to the company's fault

2.2 Subscription Refunds

Refund eligibility for subscriptions, including any partial or prorated refunds, is determined solely in accordance with Paddle’s refund policy and applicable consumer protection laws. The Company does not independently calculate or guarantee prorated refunds.

2.3 Refund Restrictions

Refunds may be restricted in the following cases:

  • When AI analysis reports have already been received (due to digital content characteristics)
  • When requesting a refund due to simple change of mind after using the service for 7 days or more
  • When service use is impossible due to customer's fault
  • Discounted amounts from events or promotions
  • When there is a record of digital services already provided and used after payment completion, refunds may be restricted in accordance with relevant laws

Where permitted by applicable law, users acknowledge that access to digital services or generated AI reports may begin immediately after purchase, and in such cases the right of withdrawal or refund may be limited.

Article 3 (Plan-Specific Notes)

The descriptions of individual products or subscription plans are provided for informational purposes only. Any eligibility for refunds, partial refunds, or billing adjustments related to specific plans is determined solely by Paddle in accordance with their policies and applicable consumer protection laws.

Article 4 (Subscription Cancellation and Refund)

4.1 Subscription Cancellation

Subscription payments are processed as follows:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time before the next billing cycle
  • When canceling a subscription, you can use the service until the current billing cycle ends
  • After cancellation, the next payment will not be automatically charged
  • Subscription cancellation can be requested at any time via customer service email and will be available through My Page in the future

Subscription cancellation stops future billing only and does not automatically entitle the user to a refund for the current billing period unless required by applicable law or Paddle’s refund policy.

4.2 Mid-subscription Cancellation

When canceling mid-subscription:

  • If a subscription is canceled during an active billing period, refunds are not guaranteed and are subject to Paddle’s refund policy and applicable consumer protection laws.
  • However, if a substantial portion of the service has already been used, the refund amount may be small

Article 5 (Service Errors and Billing Issues)

If service disruption or system errors occur, users may contact customer support. Any refunds, billing adjustments, or compensation related to such issues will be handled by Paddle in accordance with their policies and applicable laws.

Article 6 (Refund Application Method)

6.1 Application Procedure

  1. Apply for refund via customer service email (support@onnode.ai)
  2. State refund reason and order number
  3. Attach supporting documents (if necessary)
  4. Company review (within 3 business days)
  5. Refund approval and processing

Article 7 (Dispute Resolution)

In case of disputes related to refunds:

  • 1st: Consultation through customer service
  • 2nd: Korea Consumer Agency 1372 Consumer Counseling Center
  • 3rd: Electronic Commerce Dispute Mediation Committee (www.ecmc.or.kr)

Article 8 (Customer Service Information)

Refund inquiries are available through the following channels:

  • Email: support@onnode.ai
  • Operating Hours: Weekdays 08:00 - 17:00 (Excluding weekends and holidays)
  • Average Response Time: Within 24 hours

Article 9 (Policy Changes)

This refund policy may be changed in accordance with relevant laws and company policies, and changes will be announced at least 7 days in advance.

Consumer Protection: This refund policy complies with the Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce and prioritizes the protection of customer rights.
